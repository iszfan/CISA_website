CISA's Mission

Our mission is to better the environment of Chinese International students community at Rice.

Our Welcome Mixer happens at the beginning of each school year. CISA welcomes all the new international Chinese students to fit in our community.

Senior Appreciation Dinner

When graduation is coming, we have senior appreciation dinner to say farewell to our seniors.
